Lava Roguelike

A 3D browser roguelike prototype for Stormforge Abyss. Build broken relic combos, dash through enemies, survive weather disasters, and make the Abyss regret giving you loot.

  • 3D arena, third-person camera, WASD movement, mouse aim.
  • M1 slashes, dash, Thunder Dash, unlockable Hyper Beam.
  • Enemies, elites, projectiles, boss room, coins, shards, relic rewards.
  • Weather events: lightning, meteor rain, and ashfall.
  • Permanent upgrades saved in localStorage.
